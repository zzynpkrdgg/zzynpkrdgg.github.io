Experiment W

A deep dive into experimental gameplay mechanics and atmospheric world-building.

About the Game

A fast-paced 3D FPS developed in 48 hours for a Game Jam. Play as a lab subject who seizes a chance for freedom after a system glitch. Navigate a high-security facility, defeat specialized AI enemies, and lead a revolution to rescue fellow captives.

Gameplay Highlights

What I Did

Core Systems: Engineered the First-Person Controller and fluid movement mechanics using C# in Unity. Combat Mechanics: Developed the complete weapon interaction system, including shooting logic, aiming dynamics, and firing feedback. Gameplay Programming: Implemented responsive character controls to ensure a high-quality player experience under tight deadlines.
