Rocket Boost

A high-octane 3D platformer built in Unity, featuring advanced propulsion mechanics.

About the Game

Rocket Boost is a dynamic 3D platformer designed to add speed and verticality to the gameplay. It’s not just about going fast; it’s about giving the player a powerful "hit" of momentum while maintaining perfect control through challenging environments.

Gameplay Highlights

What I Did

• Fluid Physics: Programmed a momentum-based movement system in Unity to ensure satisfying character controls.
• Visual Juice: Created immersive speed effects like camera shake and FOV stretching to amplify the feeling of power.
• Strategic Balance: Implemented a resource management system to prevent spamming and add tactical depth to every jump.
